About Us

Mobile Apps Club was created in 2015 by Spencer Atkin for the purpose of teaching people how to make iPhone and Android apps. In a relaxed environment, our officers will walk you through the steps to create an app, starting with the basics and getting slightly more complicated as the year goes on. We are very friendly and like to have a good time. People of all skill levels are welcome.
